## Reuse Guidance

- Lead with `examples/12.png`, `examples/1.png`, and `examples/d1.png` when a
  viewer only has a few minutes.
- Use `examples/9.png` or `examples/2.png` when you need to prove that the
  shell is backed by real transactional workflows.
- Use `examples/4.png`, `examples/5.png`, `examples/6.png`, and `examples/7.png`
  to show breadth after the hero story is already clear.
- Keep the evidence pack aligned with the official tours in
  `docs/platform/showcase-tours.md`.
